Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about migrating to Vogue Vault.

How does the headless integration work with my current backend?

Vogue Vault acts as the presentation layer (frontend) and connects to your existing backend (Shopify, Magento, BigCommerce, or custom ERPs) via our unified GraphQL API. This allows you to keep your existing inventory and order management systems while upgrading your customer experience.

What file formats are required for 3D product previews?

We support standard GLB and glTF formats, which are optimized for web performance. If you have high-poly CAD files (like OBJ or FBX), our built-in asset pipeline will automatically compress and optimize them for fast loading on mobile devices.

Is Vogue Vault compatible with Shopify Plus?

Yes, absolutely. We have a native connector for Shopify Plus that syncs products, collections, and inventory in real-time. Checkout is handled securely via Shopify's checkout domain to ensure full compliance and trust.

How do you handle SEO with a headless setup?

Vogue Vault uses Server-Side Rendering (SSR) by default. This means search engine crawlers see fully rendered HTML content, just like a traditional site, ensuring excellent SEO performance. We also provide automated sitemap generation and meta tag management.
